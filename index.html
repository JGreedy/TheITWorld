Project Ideas

Overview:

A project idea that I had includes the creation and development of an app which helps specifically basketball players and enthusiasts who play the game. It will be an app that uses location services to help players who are looking for a basketball court to find the nearest one near them. It will also classify each court in a detailed description, by type of court, indoor or outdoor, half or full etc. The app will also tell users if other players are at any specific court and notify them if any friends that have the app are at any courts near their location.

Motivation:

This project would be interesting because basketball is one the most played sports around the world and its still growing. This app would in turn then be used by millions who are looking for free basketball courts to play on. It would also be interesting to expand the brand of basketball with the use of this app and maybe even team up with the NBA to improve the app and give users a better experience in the world of basketball. I’m also motivated by this project because I’m a player myself and know the struggles that go into finding a free basketball court to train and play on.

Description:

The app will be free and only available on mobile devices. It will have a home/dashboard which will have the main actions to be performed by the app at the ready. Main actions include, “Find Courts Nearby” where users can click to initiate the apps location services to find the nearest courts around them, “Favourite Courts” is a list of courts the user has favorited and can quickly look up to see if its free or any friends are near them. Another feature of the app could be a news tab, where users can go to see all the latest stories related to basketball, this feature could be supported by the NBA in supplying stories for the app. A side feature of the app would also be the friends section where uses can add other people that use the app and meet up to play games of basketball or just train. The important part of this app would be the “Add Court” section. This section of the app will allow users who find a basketball court that isn’t featured on the apps map to add that court to be displayed. The users application of a new court will go through a verification process. They will have to provide visual evidence of a court being situated at the specified location, and also some extra details that describe the court and its surroundings. Also a feature that is important just like the “Add Court”, is the “Check In” section of the app. This feature allows users to check in when they are near or at a basketball court that is featured on the app. This helps to notify other users of which courts are full or free and hence help them to pick courts to go to that aren’t already occupied, which is a really important part of the app and its success. The app will also allow users to either sign up with facebook, google or a special sign up through the app using an email.
